About Bright English

Bright English is a leading provider of Spanish to English tutoring services, focused on preparing students in Latin America for success in the United States and Canada.

Our experienced instructor, Sergio Vera, is a native English speaker from Canada who is passionate about helping students achieve their language learning goals. Sergio has over 10 years of experience teaching English as a Second Language and has helped countless students from Latin America improve their English proficiency.

At Bright English, we believe that learning a new language should be an engaging and rewarding experience. That's why we offer a personalized approach to each student, tailoring our lessons to their specific needs and learning styles.
